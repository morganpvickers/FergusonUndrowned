Drowned Cities

Ferguson, South Carolina

An isolated model of Ferguson. This model depicts the community and the infrastructure that now lies beneath Lake Marion. It also represents the state's attempt to flatten, submerge, and decontextualize communities in the floodpaths.

Ferguson, South Carolina - Historical Map

A 3D model of Ferguson laid atop a historic map of the town. Together, the model and the historical map depict the spatial and social context of the mill community, depicting how all buildings were in close proximity to one another and were necessary for the success of the central South Carolina timber industry.

Ferguson, South Carolina - Georeferenced

A georeferenced map of Ferguson, demonstrating how the majority of the mill community, including the villiage and its surrounds, are now under Lake Marion. The exceptions are the several mill buildings — including the kiln — which now lay in ruin on an existing island in the middle of the lake (shown in the center in green).
